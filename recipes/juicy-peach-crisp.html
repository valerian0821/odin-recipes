Juicy Peach Crisp

Description

This peach crisp recipe is made without oats, taking the simplicity of fresh peaches and turning them into a scrumptious dessert!

Ingredients

  • 6 fresh peaches - peeled, pitted, and sliced
  • ½ teaspoon almond extract
  • 1 cup all-purpose flour
  • 1 cup white sugar
  • ¼ cup brown sugar
  • ½ teaspoon ground cinnamon
  • ¼ teaspoon salt
  • ½ cup unsalted butter

Steps

  1. Preheat the oven to 375 degrees F (190 degrees C). Grease an 8-inch square baking dish.
  2. Place peach slices into the bottom of the baking dish and sprinkle with almond extract.
  3. Combine flour, sugar, brown sugar, cinnamon, and salt in a bowl. Cut the butter into the flour mixture with a pastry cutter until the mixture resembles crumbs; sprinkle over peaches in an even layer.
  4. Bake in the preheated oven until peaches are bubbling and topping is browned, about 45 minutes.
